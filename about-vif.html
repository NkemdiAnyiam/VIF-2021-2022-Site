About the Viz Industry Fair (VIF)

The Viz Industry Fair is an opportunity for students interested in Animation, Game Development, and Graphics & Interactive Technologies to both connect with prospective employers working in high level positions in some of today's leading Industries and receive professional feedback on their work.

Because of continuing concerns with the Pandemic, we will be holding a Virtual Fair this year. The Fair will take place Friday, February 25, 2022. Times are TBD, and will be posted when finalized.

The Viz Industry Fair is run by a student run committee. These dedicated students, with focuses in Game Development, Animation, and Interactive Media, such as UI/UX and web design, reach out to companies, create the Fair content and website, and run the social media campaign of the V.I.F.

Along with this year's fair, The V.I.F. Committee has also put together several other events throughout the school year. These include industry speakers, portfolio reviews, mock interviews, and resume reviews.
