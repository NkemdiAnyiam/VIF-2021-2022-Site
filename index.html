Welcome to the 2022 Viz Industry Fair!

This event is an opportunity for students interested in Animation, Gaming, Graphics, and Interactive technologies to connect with prospective employers working in high level positions in some of today's leading industries and receive professional feedback on their work.

Because of continuing concerns with the Pandemic, we will be holding a Virtual Fair this year. The Fair will take place Friday, February 25, 2022, 10am–4pm.

The Viz Industry Fair is run by a student run committee. These dedicated students, with focuses in Game Development, Animation, and Interactive Media, such as UI/UX and web design, reach out to companies, create the Fair content and website, and run the social media campaign of the V.I.F.

Along with this year's fair, The V.I.F. Committee has also put together several other events throughout the school year. These include industry speakers, portfolio reviews, mock interviews, and resume reviews.

Register below by Thursday, February 17, 11:59 pm. We look forward to seeing you at our many events and the fair!
